Dragonfly

Dragonfly is a web browser built with webkitgtk. It is designed to be fast and simple to use, and is inspired by projects such as surf, midori, and leafpad.

Dependencies

Dragonfly requires gtk+ 2.0 and webkigtk 1.0.
